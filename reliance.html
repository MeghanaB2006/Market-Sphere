About Reliance Industries

Reliance Industries Limited is India's largest private sector company and a Fortune Global 500 company. It operates across energy, petrochemicals, oil & gas, telecom, and retail sectors. The company has transformed from a textiles and polyester company to an integrated energy and materials company and further to a technology platform company. RIL's major subsidiaries include Jio Platforms and Reliance Retail, making it a key player in India's digital revolution and retail transformation.
